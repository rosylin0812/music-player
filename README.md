### Music Player for Fan Bai (Buddhist Chanting) Practice

Link : https://fan-bai-player.vercel.app/

This modern web app is a dedicated music player for Fan Bai/ Buddhist Chanting practice.

It lets you organize songs and playlists exactly how you want with simple drag-and-drop, works fully offline, and saves everything permanently on your device. 

Clean, professional, and focused, it replaces scattered chat apps with one powerful space for all your practice audiosâ€”so you spend less time searching and more timeÂ practicing.

## ğŸš€ How to Install as a Mobile App (PWA)

This project is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, meaning you can install it directly from your browserâ€”no app store needed!

Once the music player is deployed to a live link (like the Vercel URL above), follow these steps to install it on your mobile device:

### For iPhone / iPad Users (using Safari):

1.  **Open Safari:** Navigate to the live website link (e.g., `https://fanbia.vercel.app/`).
2.  **Tap Share:** Find the **Share button** ($\square \uparrow$) at the bottom of the screen.
3.  **Add to Home Screen:** Scroll down and select the option **"Add to Home Screen."**
4.  **Confirm:** Tap **"Add."**
    * *Result:* The app icon will appear on your home screen and open in a full-screen, dedicated app window.

### For Android Users (using Chrome):

1.  **Open Chrome:** Navigate to the live website link.
2.  **Tap Menu:** Tap the **Menu button** ($\vdots$) in the top-right corner.
3.  **Install App:** Select **"Install app"** (or sometimes **"Add to Home Screen"**).
4.  **Confirm:** Tap **"Install"** or **"Add."**
    * *Result:* The app icon will appear in your app drawer and on your home screen, running like a native app.

### Key Benefit: Offline Use

Once installed, the app works **completely offline**! You can:

* Upload your Buddhist chanting audio files.
* Manage and edit all your playlists.
* Play all local songs *without* needing an internet connection.
